Loftia's Hydroponic System

Loftia is a green-future-style MMORPG that is scheduled to release in 2024. One of the kickstarter rewards is this pretty skin for an in-game hydroponic setup, and I want to have one in real life. Here is a sneak peek on how the project is going. The system will be entirely 3D printable on a Prusa MK3 and plan to provide the files here on my website and on printables.com.

TPU Lens Caps

Designed a series of 13 lens caps for 24/7 Electronics. They are printed in-house in TPU, allowing a snug fit for many different camera and lens sizes. Shown here is the 55mm model.

Brass Ultrasonic Probe

A sonication probe that I made out of brass on a beautiful old manual lathe. It was used to take a liquid-metal catalyst and break it into tiny spheres in a solvent, making a liquid-metal-and-solvent vinaigrette. This drastically increases the surface area of the catalyst for the formation of graphene sheets on the surface of the metal. I worked full-time on this carbon sequastration project in the summer of 2022.

Selen Tatsuki Costume Element

This is the charm that hangs off of Selen Tatsuki's belt in her V2 model. The design inspired me to create a 3d-printable model that can be used for cosplay. I recommend printing it in two identical halves that fit together, and then electroplating the print in silver.

Mochi Donut Dispenser with Plunger

If you're making mochi donuts at home, it gets fiddly. Instead of just being able to *plop* them into the hot oil with a machine, you have to painstakingly roll each sphere and stick them together in a ring. This is a low-budget, at-home version of the big mochi donut machine, complete with a plunger. The six holes on the bottom extrude the spheres, and the donut can be released by swiftly rotating the plunger. I havent tested it yet. If you want to be the first, download the plunger here and the cup here.
