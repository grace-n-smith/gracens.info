Accessible tutorials for building websites

It took me way longer than I would like to admit to build this website for myself. Part of the reason it took this long is that most tutorials online are either designed for those with web development background or are trying to sell a service. Grace's Guide to the Internet will be a complete tutorial for deploying static sites for free, with no previous experience. Most importantly though, I have no ulterior motives.


What you see to the right is an embed of the guide on this website for quick viewing.
