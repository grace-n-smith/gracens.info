Brass Ultrasonic Probe

A sonication probe that I made out of brass on a manual lathe. It was used to take a liquid-metal catalyst and break it into tiny spheres in a solvent, making a liquid-metal-and-solvent vinaigrette.

Selen Tatsuki's silver charm

This is the charm that hangs off of Selen Tatsuki's belt in her V2 model. The design inspired me to create a 3d-printable model that can be used for cosplay. I recommend printing it in two identical halves that fit together, and then electroplating the print in silver.

TPU Lens Caps

Designed a series of 13 lens caps for 24/7 Electronics. They are printed in-house in TPU, allowing a snug fit for many different camera and lens sizes. Shown here is the 55mm model.
